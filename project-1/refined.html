OUR PARTNERS

Our mission at Teleo is to love our neighbors. One of the ways we do this is through the products we bring in, having them go directly back into the St. Louis community. We do this by purchasing from many different local businesses. Check them out!

ROLLING FARMS

All of our milk comes from this incredible family-owned milk farm in Greenville, IL. They have the best milk and the cutest cows!

WEIDNER FARM

We use this local honey for our popular latte, the Honey Bear. This sweet bee farmer is located out in Chesterfield, MO.

BIG HEART TEA CO.

Big heart is an awesome local company here in St. Louis that puts so much intentionality into using direct trade and organic herbs to create their tea offerings.

RECLAIM RENEW

This local furniture store here in Kirkwood uses all reclaimed wood. Our tables, chairs, shelves and kids room wood is all from Reclaim Renew.

EARTH SPROUT

All of the beautiful plants and landscaping you see here at the shop comes from this awesome small family business. They come in and do both the design and installation!
