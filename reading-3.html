When I think of new emergent technology, I tend to think of cyborgs, which I think tends to be where it trends these days. It's more focused on how we can adapt our bodies and our senses to interpret the world around us. However, the thing I think about is how a lot of sci-fi movies or futuristic shows often warn about if our soceity turns like: reliant on technology instead of trusting our own selves, and our own senses. I think that's why the room model is much more interesting, to me and to the author, because we really haven't conceived all the ways we can adjust the environment to help us, rather than adjusting our own selves.
